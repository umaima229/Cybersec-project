CYBER THREATS

Protecting Your Digital Assets Expertly

Cyber threats are harmful actions aimed at damaging, stealing, or disrupting digital systems, networks, or data. They come in various forms like viruses, malware, phishing, ransomware, and hacking. These threats can target individuals, businesses, or governments. Attackers often try to access personal or sensitive information without permission. Cyber threats can lead to data loss, identity theft, or financial damage. Staying aware and following safety practices helps protect against such attacks. Using strong passwords, antivirus software, and avoiding suspicious links are key steps.

SAFETY TIPS

  • Use Strong, Unique Passwords
    Combine uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols — never reuse passwords.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
    Add an extra layer of login security using OTPs or biometrics.
  • Be Cautious of Suspicious Links & Emails
    Avoid clicking on unknown links or attachments — common phishing trick.
  • Keep Software & Devices Updated
    Always install updates to fix security holes.
  • Avoid Using Public Wi-Fi for Sensitive Tasks
    Use VPN or avoid logging into accounts on public networks.
