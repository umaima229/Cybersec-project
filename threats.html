CyberSec Threats

Unmasking the Hidden Dangers of the Digital World

Phishing

Fake emails or websites designed to trick users into giving up personal info. Always double-check links before clicking!

Malware

Malicious software like viruses and spyware that can damage or steal your data. Keep your antivirus up to date.

Social Engineering

Tricking people into revealing confidential data using manipulation and trust. Stay alert, not just secure!

DDoS Attacks

Overloading websites/servers to make them crash. These attacks are disruptive but preventable with proper defenses.

Ransomware

A dangerous malware that locks your data and demands payment. Backups and strong firewalls are key to prevention.

Zero-Day Exploits

Attacks on software flaws unknown to developers. Regular updates and security patches reduce this risk.
